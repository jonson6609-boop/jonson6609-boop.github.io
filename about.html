About The Small Fix

The Small Fix is a home for practical, low-effort improvements — the kind you can actually finish before your coffee gets cold. No 5 a.m. routines, no expensive gear, no 40-step systems. Just small, specific changes to money, home, focus, and everyday wellbeing.

What we believe

Most advice fails not because it's wrong, but because it's too big to fit into a real week. We look for the smallest version of an idea that still works, and we write it down clearly enough that you can start today.

Who writes here

Our small team of contributors focuses on ideas they've actually tested in their own lives before writing about them. Every post aims to leave you with one concrete thing to try, not a list to feel guilty about.
